Rescues

For half a century, World Wildlife Fund Canada has worked to protect the future of nature. WWF-Canada is the country’s largest international conservation organization, with the active support of hundreds of thousands of Canadians. Our goal is to protect wildlife and find good homes for good pets. We have a variety of loveable pets who you will be happy to call a friend.

Ferret Bueller

Ferret Bueller is a friendly white male ferret who spends his time stealing shoes and hiding keys. If you're not over here in fifteen minutes, you can find a new best friend.

Al Pacachino

Al Pacachino is not your typical alpaca, he enjoys spitting, but not the wet kind, spitting those sweet, sweet rhymes. He does not need bodyguards, he's from the South Bronx.

Peter Panda

Peter Panda is a little fireball, he loves to lounge aroun and eat whatever he gets his paws on. He's easy to love, all you need is faith, trust and a little pixie dust.

Puff Daddy

Puff Daddy Puffin' is a happy camper, he makes his own fire because his rhymes are so lit. Can't nobody take his pride, can't nobody hold him down.
